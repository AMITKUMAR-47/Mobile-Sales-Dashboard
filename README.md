# Mobile Sales Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze mobile phone sales performance, customer purchasing trends, and regional market insights. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales metrics, product performance, and business growth through data-driven visualizations and KPI tracking.

## Project Overview

The Mobile Sales Dashboard helps businesses monitor sales performance, identify top-selling mobile brands and models, understand customer preferences, and evaluate regional sales distribution. The dashboard enables informed decision-making by transforming raw sales data into meaningful insights.

## Features

* Total Sales, Quantity Sold, and Revenue Analysis
* Top-Selling Mobile Brands and Models
* Monthly Sales Trend Analysis
* Region-wise and City-wise Sales Performance
* Customer Purchase Behavior Analysis
* Brand-wise Revenue Distribution
* Interactive Filters and Slicers
* Executive Summary and Key Insights

## Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* Microsoft Excel

## Key KPIs

* Total Revenue
* Total Units Sold
* Total Transactions
* Average Selling Price
* Top Performing Brand
* Monthly Sales Growth

## Business Insights

* Identifies the highest revenue-generating mobile brands.
* Tracks monthly sales trends and seasonal demand patterns.
* Analyzes regional sales performance and customer preferences.
* Highlights top-selling products and their contribution to revenue.
* Supports strategic decision-making through interactive visualizations.

This dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of mobile sales data, helping stakeholders understand market trends, optimize product strategies, and improve overall business performance through data-driven decision-making.
